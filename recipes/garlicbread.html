Garlic Bread Recipe

Garlic Bread

Garlic bread stems from bruschetta, which appeared in Italy around the 15th century, and can be traced back to Ancient Rome.

If you would like to learn more about its history, please follow this link

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 baguette or French bread, cut lengthwise
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 1/4 c. grated Parmesan

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â° and line a large baking sheet with foil. Make garlic compound butter: In a small bowl, mix softened butter with garlic, salt, and parsley until will combined.
  2. Spread cut sides of bread liberally with garlic butter. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  3. Bake until bread is toasty and golden at the edges, about 10 minutes.
